# Getting Involved

Would you like to help us make the Dinghy even better? There are many ways in which you can get involved, make a difference and help the Dinghy development team to make it more fun to use? We really appreciate and welcome contributions and you can help us by:

1. helping other users, Q&A or discussing features on the [Github Discussions](https://github.com/dinghydev/dinghy/discussions)
1. reporting or fixing issues you encounter in our [Github Issues](https://github.com/dinghydev/dinghy/issues)
1. privately discuss, fix, and publish information about security vulnerabilities in Dinghy source code at [Github Security](https://github.com/dinghydev/dinghy/security)
1. writing a blog post about some Dinghy feature or your Dinghy usage. Please [let us known](./contact) and will share with the community.
1. and of course, by [contributing bug fixes or new features](./contribute/contributing-code)
